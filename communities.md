
# below are some communities for beginners to check out

## general

[bsides](https://bsides.org/) is a community for hosting cyber events / conferences

[burbsec](https://burbsec.com/) is a community for infosec meetups with a variety of location-specific groups

flipper has a [community page](https://flipper.net/pages/community) on their site linking to their blog, discord, repos, and more

https://discord.com/invite/bhis

https://discord.com/invite/momentum

https://forums.kali.org/

## mn specific

[gopherhack](https://gopherhack.com/) is a hacking & CTF club at the University of Minnesota

[free geek twin cities](https://www.freegeektwincities.org/) is a community of tech enthusiasts (and a cool place to volunteer)

[def con's mn spin-off](https://dc612.org/index.php/about/)
